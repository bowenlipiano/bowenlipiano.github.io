    PIANIST / PIANO TEACHER
    Bowen Li is an award-winning pianist originally from Australia. He has been a finalist in national and international music competitions such as the 2MBS FM Australian Young Virtuosi Award, the Sydney Eisteddfod Allison and Henderson Scholarship, the Glenn Gould School Concerto Competition and the Prix d’Orford Music Award and has been the winner of the Sydney Eisteddfod Concerto Competition and the Ryde Eisteddfod Scholarship Award. Bowen will also be participating as a finalist in the upcoming Australian National Piano Award in October 2022. Bowen has also performed as a solo pianist in Australia, North America and Europe and his performances have been featured on classical music radio stations in Australia.

    A student of the famous pianist André Laplante, Bowen completed his Bachelor of Music and Performance Diploma from the Glenn Gould School of the Royal Conservatory of Music in Toronto, his Masters of Music from the University of Montreal and completed the prestigious Musical Perfection Certificate (Certificat de stage de perfectionnement en musique) at the Montreal Conservatory of Music. He has also studied with other world-renowned teachers such as Dang Thai Son, Leon Fleisher, and Robert McDonald.

    As a teacher, Bowen has over 10 years of teaching experience and has taught students ranging from children as young as 6 to adult beginners to advanced student pianists. Bowen strongly believes in the potential of every single student and the innate musical ability of every human being. His teaching philosophy is that any student given the right individual attention and personalized training has the ability to become a capable and accomplished musician.
