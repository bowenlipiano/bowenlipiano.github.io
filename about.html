    • Bowen Li
    PIANIST / PIANO TEACHER
    Bowen is an award winning pianist from Australia. He recently won the Leona and Bruce Sterling Prize at the 2022 Australian National Piano Awards. He has also won the Sydney Eisteddfod Piano Concerto Competition and been a finalist in competitions such as the 2MBS FM Young Virtuosi Award and the Prix d’Orford.

    Bowen obtained his Master of Music from the University of Montreal and his Bachelor of Music and Performance Diploma from the Glenn Gould School of the Royal Conservatory of Music. He also holds an Artist Diploma from the Montreal Conservatory. Bowen has been a student of famed Canadian pianists André Laplante and Jamie Parker. He also studied with many of the top pedagogues in the world such as Leon Fleisher, Robert McDonald, and Dang Thai Son.

    Bowen has been teaching piano for over ten years and has taught students as young as 3 and as advanced as undergraduate university level students. Bowen’s students have completed exams from many different music examination organizations often with high marks. These organizations include the Royal Conservatory of Music (RCM), the McGill Conservatory, and the Australian Music Examination Board (AMEB). Bowen’s students have also been award top prizes at competitions such as the Kiwanis Music Festival.

    Bowen has been invited to teach master classes for intermediate and advanced students at the University of Toronto. He currently works there as a piano instructor for the Music Minor program.
